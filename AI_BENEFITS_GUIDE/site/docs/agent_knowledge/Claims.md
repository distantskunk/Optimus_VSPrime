## Reimbursement Notes

- Out-of-network exam reimbursement is capped by plan.
- David Chen's $210 exam paid out at $45 because his plan caps out-of-network exam reimbursement at $45.
- Members submit an itemized receipt to be reimbursed.
- In the prototype, treat receipt submission as documented but out-of-flow; the form does not need to be built.
- Reimbursement typically processes in 30 days.
- Pending claims older than 30 days are a reason to escalate.
