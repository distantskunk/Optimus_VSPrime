## Common Features Across All Four Plans

- WellVision Exam every calendar year. Focus on eye and overall wellness.
- Essential Medical Eye Care available as needed ($20 per exam).
- Prescription glasses coverage: frame + lenses, with copay per plan.
- Contacts in lieu of glasses: one or the other per benefit period.
- VSP EasyOptions: member can pick one upgrade, such as extra frame allowance, premium/custom progressives, light-reactive, anti-glare, or extra contact allowance.
- VSP LightCare: allowance for ready-made non-prescription sunglasses or blue light glasses, instead of Rx glasses or contacts.
- 20% off additional eyewear from a VSP provider within 12 months of last exam.
- 15% off Laser Vision Correction at contracted facilities.
